About Project

CogniPath is an AI-Powered Career Intelligence Platform created for the Infosys Springboard Virtual Internship. It demonstrates authentication, profile management, resume analysis, skill-gap insights, career recommendations, analytics, reports, admin controls, and deployment-ready frontend architecture.
